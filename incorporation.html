Incorporation & Governance

Our organizational structure and management

Legal Status

Registered & Accredited Non-Profit

RNF is registered as a non-profit organization under the Non-Governmental Organizations Coordination Act. We received our official registration in 2013 and have maintained good standing with all regulatory authorities since our inception.

  • NGO Coordination Act
  • Tax exemption requirements
  • Employment laws
  • Financial reporting standards
  • Data protection regulations
Certifications & Accreditations:
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certification for Quality Management Systems
  • NGO Council Code of Conduct Compliance Certification
  • Global Giving Vetted Organization Status

Policies and Procedures

We have established comprehensive policies and procedures to guide our operations and ensure consistency, quality, and accountability. Key policies include:

Child Protection Policy

Comprehensive guidelines to ensure the safety and wellbeing of all minors involved in our programs.

Financial Management Policy

Procedures for transparent and accountable management of financial resources.

Human Resources Policy

Guidelines for staff recruitment, development, and management.

Monitoring & Evaluation Policy

Framework for assessing the effectiveness and impact of our programs.

Partnership Policy

Criteria and procedures for establishing and managing partnerships.

Whistleblower Policy

Protections for those who report misconduct or ethical concerns.

Financial Transparency

We are committed to financial transparency and accountability. Our annual reports and financial statements are publicly available, and we maintain the highest standards of fiscal responsibility.
