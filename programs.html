Programs & Initiatives

Our strategies for creating meaningful impact

Our programs and initiatives translate our mission into action, addressing the specific needs of teenage girls through evidence-based approaches that create lasting change in their lives and communities.

We believe in a holistic approach that addresses the interconnected challenges girls face. Our programs are designed with input from girls themselves, ensuring they are relevant, effective, and sustainable. We begin with thorough needs assessment and community engagement, design evidence-based interventions, implement with strong support systems, rigorously monitor and evaluate, and develop strategies for scale and sustainability.

Throughout this process, we emphasize participatory approaches that involve girls, families, and communities in program design, implementation, and evaluation.

Empowerment Ages 12-18

Girls Empowerment Clubs

Safe spaces in schools where girls develop leadership skills, build confidence, and learn about their rights through peer-to-peer learning.

  • 95% increase in self-reported confidence levels
  • 87% improvement in academic performance
  • Enhanced peer support networks
Learn more
Mentorship Ages 14-22

Mentorship Program

One-on-one and group mentoring connecting young women with successful professionals in various fields.

  • 90% of participants pursue higher education
  • 85% report improved career clarity
  • Strong professional networks established
Learn more
Education Ages 10-18

Girls in STEM Initiative

Hands-on workshops and training in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics to inspire the next generation of female innovators.

  • 75% pursue STEM subjects in higher education
  • Increased interest in technology careers
  • Improved problem-solving skills
Learn more
Health Ages 12-19

Health & Wellness Program

Comprehensive health education covering reproductive health, nutrition, mental wellness, and access to healthcare services.

  • 80% improvement in health knowledge
  • Better mental health outcomes
  • Reduced teen pregnancy rates
Learn more
Protection Ages 12-18

Safe Spaces Initiative

Community centers providing protection, counseling, and support services for girls at risk of violence or exploitation.

  • 60% reduction in reported cases of gender-based violence
  • Improved access to legal and psychological support
  • Strengthened community protection networks
Learn more
Digital Inclusion Ages 14-19

Digital Girls

Bridges the digital gender gap by providing teenage girls with access to technology, digital literacy skills, and opportunities in STEM fields.

  • 95% digital literacy achievement
  • Access to technology resources
  • Recognition through awards including educational scholarships
