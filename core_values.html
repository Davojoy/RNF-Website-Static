Core Values

The principles that guide our work and organization

At RNF, our core values are not just words on a page. They are deeply embedded in our organizational culture and reflected in everything we do, from our program design to our interactions with the girls we serve, their communities, and our partners.

Our Core Values

Respect & Dignity

We treat everyone with respect and uphold their inherent dignity. We value diverse perspectives, cultures, and backgrounds, and we ensure that all voices are heard and valued.

Equity & Inclusion

We are committed to promoting equity and inclusion in all aspects of our work. We actively seek to reach the most marginalized and vulnerable girls, and we challenge systems and practices that perpetuate discrimination.

Integrity & Transparency

We conduct our operations with the highest standards of integrity and transparency. We are accountable to the communities we serve, our partners, and our donors, and we use resources responsibly.

Innovation & Learning

We embrace innovation and continuous learning. We regularly evaluate our programs, adapt our approaches based on evidence and feedback, and seek creative solutions to complex challenges.

Collaboration & Partnership

We believe in the power of collaboration. We work closely with communities, government agencies, civil society organizations, and other stakeholders to leverage collective expertise and resources for maximum impact.

Excellence & Impact

We strive for excellence in all our programs and initiatives. We are results-oriented and committed to creating measurable, sustainable impact in the lives of the girls we serve and their communities.

How We Live Our Values

In Our Programs

  • We design programs based on thorough needs assessments and with input from the girls we serve.
  • We create safe, inclusive spaces where all girls feel welcome and valued.
  • We adapt our approaches to accommodate different learning styles and needs.
  • We regularly evaluate our programs and make improvements based on feedback and results.
  • We ensure that our curriculum and materials are culturally relevant and age-appropriate.

In Our Community Engagement

  • We engage with communities as partners, not just beneficiaries.
  • We respect local customs and cultural practices while advocating for girls' rights.
  • We build the capacity of community members to support girls' education and development.
  • We ensure transparency in our communications and decision-making processes.
  • We celebrate and amplify community successes and contributions.

In Our Organization

  • We maintain a diverse team and board that reflects the communities we serve.
  • We create a supportive work environment where staff can grow and thrive.
  • We practice transparent financial management and regularly share reports with stakeholders.
  • We invest in ongoing professional development and learning opportunities for our team.
  • We make decisions through inclusive processes that consider multiple perspectives.
